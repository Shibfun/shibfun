Welcome to ShibFun!

Hey, Shiba pal! ShibFun Bot lets you trade tokens on Shibarium, earn HAM rewards, and have a tail-wagging good time. Your username, Loading..., is your key to the fun!

Get Started

1. Bark at @ShibFun_bot in Telegram to join the pack.

2. Your username unlocks wallets and trades.

3. Switch languages with the buttons above. Woof woof!

Paw-some Features

Shiba Wallet

- Dig a Wallet: Get one with /generatewallet in private chat.

- Check Wallet: Peek at it with /mywallet.

- Count Treats: See BONE and tokens with /balance.

Trading Fun

- Buy Tokens: Grab some with /buy BONE of (e.g., /buy 1 BONE of WOOF).

- Sell Tokens: Swap back with /sell (e.g., /sell 100 WOOF).

- Share Bones: Toss to friends with @username tip (e.g., @Friend tip 0.5 BONE).

HAM Rewards

- Daily Bark: Say mint in a group for 10 HAM every 20 hours.

- Fetch Pals: Use /invite to share a link and earn 1 HAM per join.

- HAM Treats: Share with /redpacket (e.g., /redpacket 100 5).

- Token Treats: Spread tokens with / (e.g., /WOOF 10 5).

Your Dog Tag

- Profile: Check HAM, wallet, and invites with /myinfo.

- Add Wallet: Use /setwallet

for external wallets.
